Florida

History

Florida was inhabited by Native Americans, colonized by Spain, became US territory, and achieved statehood in 1845. Its warm climate attracted settlers, and tourism and agriculture flourished over time.

Famous Places

Walt Disney World

Walt Disney World, located in Orlando, is the largest and most visited theme park resort in the world. It includes Magic Kingdom, Epcot, Disney’s Hollywood Studios, and Disney’s Animal Kingdom.

Everglades National Park

Everglades National Park is a unique ecosystem with vast wetlands, mangroves, and diverse wildlife. Visitors can enjoy airboat tours, bird watching, and eco-adventures.

Visit Everglades
Everglades National Park

Miami Art Deco District

The Miami Art Deco District in South Beach is known for pastel-colored buildings, historic hotels, and vibrant nightlife, representing a major cultural and architectural attraction.

Famous Artists & Celebrities

Tom Petty

Tom Petty, born in Gainesville, Florida, was a rock musician and leader of Tom Petty and the Heartbreakers, known for hits like "Free Fallin'" and "American Girl".
Tom Petty

Gloria Estefan

Gloria Estefan, a Cuban-American singer from Miami, is famous for Latin pop hits and has won multiple Grammy Awards.
Gloria Estefan

Wifredo Lam

Wifredo Lam, an influential Cuban artist who spent time in Florida, is known for his modernist and surrealist artworks blending Afro-Cuban culture and European modernism.
Wifredo Lam
